Spatchcock Roasted Chicken

Spatchcock a whole chicken for a quick and even roast. Learn how to spatchcock a chicken from this video:



I am using a recipe found here.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole 4-pound chicken
  • 2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 lemons, thinly sliced, divided
  • 6 small shallots, peeled and quartered lengthwise

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Rub chicken with oil. Season liberally with salt and pepper.
  3. Brush 1 tablespoon oil in the center of a rimmed baking sheet.
  4. Place half the lemon slices in a single layer on top of oil.
  5. Place chicken, skin side up, on lemons.
  6. Carefully loosen skin from flesh of breast and thighs with your fingers.
  7. Slide remaining lemon slices and any other herbs under skin in a single layer.
  8. Roast chicken 20 minutes. Toss shallots with oil, and scatter around chicken.
  9. 25 to 30 minutes more. Breast should reach 165 degrees.
  10. Place chicken on cutting board, rest for 10 minutes. Pour remaining juices, carve and serve.
The chicken can be cooled and used for chicken salad. Can store for 2 days.
